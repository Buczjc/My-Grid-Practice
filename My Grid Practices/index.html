My Grid Practices

Eren Yeager

Attack On Titan

Eren Yeager, protagonist of "Attack on Titan," joins the military to fight Titans and seeks freedom for his people, using increasingly extreme methods.

" You'll never know, if you're not the one who's continuing to take that path... unless you keep moving forward. "

Ri Shin

Kingdom

Ri Shin, protagonist of "Kingdom," dreams of becoming the greatest general to bring peace and unity to China during the Warring States period.

" The most regretful thing there is when your dreams end as nothing but an illusion! "

Thorfinn

Vinland Saga

Thorfinn, protagonist of "Vinland Saga," transitions from seeking revenge to pursuing a peaceful life in the legendary Vinland.

" Warriors don't have any regrets. We do what we have to do without hesitation "

Dio Brando

JoJo Bizarre Adventure

Dio Brando, main antagonist of "JoJo's Bizarre Adventure," uses a cursed mask to become a vampire and seeks to dominate the Joestar family.

" The more carefully you scheme, the more unexpected events come along. "

Yujiro Hanma

Baki

Yujiro Hanma, central antagonist in "Baki the Grappler," seeks to prove his dominance by fighting the strongest opponents, including his son Baki.

" I will not rest until all challengers have been defeated. "
